# Backup Training Plan (February)

Notes: Use this plan when you need to adapt training based on sleep, HRV, or illness.

## 1) Restricted sleep + lower HRV (no meaningful trend change)

### **B1.A** Activity
Treadmill / AirBike / Stationary bike.

### **B1.B** Protocol (aerobic, low + moderate intensity)
30 minutes total.

4 rounds:
- 2:30 easy @ HR 115–125
- 5:00 steady/moderate @ HR 158–165

Optional: add a 5th round if you have time/energy.

### **B1.C** Heart Rate Targets
- Easy: HR 115–125
- Moderate: HR 158–165

## 2) Low HRV not caused by restricted sleep (e.g., ~20 ms drop)

### **B2.A** Activity
Resistance training (full-body circuit).

### **B2.B** Protocol
- Circuit of 5 movements (slow tempo).
- 3–4 rounds total.
- Time per round: up to ~6 minutes.
- Rest between rounds: up to 3 minutes.

### **B2.C** Exercises (1 round)
1) Reverse dumbbell lunges from a deficit — DB **2×8 kg** — **10 reps** — slow tempo
2) Flat dumbbell bench press — DB **2×15 kg** — **10 reps** — slow tempo
3) Lying leg + hip raises — **30 reps** — slow tempo
4) Dumbbell overhead press — DB **2×12 kg** — **10 reps** — slow tempo
5) Supinating dumbbell biceps curl — DB **2×8 kg** — **10 reps** — slow tempo

## 3) Illness

### **B3.A** Action
Skip the training session (rest and recover).
